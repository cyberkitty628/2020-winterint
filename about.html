About

Steven Tucker (@tuckedvisuals) is a photographer/videographer based in Oakland, CA. Growing up, he enjoyed capturing photographs with disposable cameras and developing film into physical images, which led to photography classes in high school. Today, photography and cinematography are a means for him to travel back in time and relive cherished moments while exploring his personal creativity. He aims to offer a unique, custom-tailored experience to each of his clients. His expertise includes fashion, lifestyle, portraits, pregnancy, cosplay and more.
